A Brief About Me:

Hey! My name is Will Taylor. I grew up in Spokane, Washington, and I’m currently studying at the University of Washington. Along with that, I am a co-founder of and general manager for Flinch eSports, which is a StarCraft II team and eSports organization based in Australia.

As to Flinch, I have been a professional StarCraft II player since 2013. After coming to college, I realized that I no longer had the time to dedicate to specifically competing. So, I instead took everything I had learned from competing and turned it to creating an organization to (ideally) bolster the global StarCraft scene, to facilitate other players continued ability to make a career out of professional play, and to act as a part of professional StarCraft's platform.

With that in mind, I’m intending to study Computer Science with a focus in either Machine Learning / Artificial Intelligence or data sciences, and am currently looking to expand my work experience in the general programming field.
